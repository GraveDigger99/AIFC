Services

Litigation

The AIFC Court provides a forum for commercial litigation applying common law procedure with modern, quick and cost-effective case management. Decisions in cases before the AIFC Court are given by the highest international standard judges renowned for their absolute independence, impartiality, integrity, unconditional application of the rule of law, and incorruptibility.

Enforcement

Simplified Labour and Visa Regimes Businesses enjoy a simplified labour regime and visa-related procedures that can be obtained on a one stop-shop basis.

Rights of Audience

World-class Regulation Founded upon the English Common Law, the AIFC Regulator - AFSA upholds top international standards for regulating financial services, drawing from guidelines endorsed by IOSCO, IAIS, BCBS, IFSB, OECD, FATF, and other reputable organizations.
